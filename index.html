Our Story
From Bean to Bar — Crafted with Love

VELVET FLOW was born in 2010 from a simple obsession: finding the perfect chocolate. Our founder travelled across Belgium, Switzerland and Ecuador sourcing the world's finest cacao, learning centuries-old techniques from master chocolatiers.


Today, every piece we craft is a tribute to that journey — made by hand, infused with care, and delivered to your door with uncompromising quality. We believe chocolate is not just a treat; it's an emotion.
